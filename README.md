## Principles Behind These Templates

**Simple > Complete.** A PRD nobody reads is worse than no PRD. These templates are deliberately short.

**Document the why, not the what.** The decision log matters more than the feature list. Future-you will know what you built; you might forget why.

**Assumptions are liabilities.** Every template ends with open questions. Untracked assumptions become surprises.

---

## How to Use

1. Copy the template into your repo or Notion
2. Delete sections that don't apply — don't fill in placeholders
3. Update in real-time as decisions change (a stale doc is worse than none)
