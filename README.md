> ### 📋 **Core Frameworks**
>
> #### 1. RICE Prioritization Framework
> Rapidly evaluate feature requests and prioritize your roadmap.
>
> **File:** `frameworks/RICE.md`
>
> **Quick Reference:**
> ```
> RICE = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort
>
> - Reach: How many users affected? (# per quarter)
> - Impact: How much does this help each user? (3=massive, 2=high, 1=medium, 0.5=small)
> - Confidence: How confident are you? (100%, 80%, 50%)
> - Effort: How many person-months to build?
> ```
>
> #### 2. Problem-Solution Framework
> Structure your thinking before building.
>
> **File:** `frameworks/Problem-Solution-Framework.md`
>
> **Components:**
> - Problem Statement (user + pain point)
> - - Why Now? (what's changed?)
>   - - Solution Overview (1-sentence elevator pitch)
>     - - Success Metrics (how will you know it works?)
>       - - Risks & Mitigations (what could go wrong?)
>        
>         - #### 3. Opportunity Scoring Matrix
>         - Evaluate multiple dimensions of value.
>        
>         - **File:** `frameworks/Opportunity-Scoring.md`
>        
>         - **Dimensions:**
> - Desirability (do users want it?)
> - - Feasibility (can we build it?)
>   - - Viability (does it make business sense?)
>     - - Timeline (when can we launch?)
>      
>       - #### 4. Decision Log Template
>       - Document the "why" behind your decisions.
>      
>       - **File:** `templates/Decision-Log.md`
>      
>       - **Includes:**
> - Decision Statement
> - - Context & Options Considered
>   - - Chosen Path
>     - - Reasoning
>       - - Expected Outcomes
>         - - Assumptions to Validate

>
> #### Metric Definitions by Stage
> **File:** `frameworks/Metrics-by-Stage.md`
>
> **Early Stage (Product-Market Fit)**
> - Activation Rate
> - - Retention (D7, D30)
>   - - Referral Rate
>     - - NPS (Net Promoter Score)
>      
>       - **Growth Stage**
>       - - CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

>           - - Payback Period

>              
>               - **Mature Stage**
>               - - Churn Rate
>                 - - Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

>                     - - Win/Loss Rates

> - [ ] ## How to Use This Toolkit
>
> - [ ] 1. **Read the Framework** - Start with the overview document
> - [ ] 2. **Adapt the Template** - Most templates have guidance for customization
> - [ ] 3. **Share & Collaborate** - Work through these as a team
> - [ ] 4. **Document Your Thinking** - Keep decision logs for future reference
> - [ ] 5. **Iterate** - These are starting points, not gospel truth
>
> - [ ] ---
>
> - [ ] ## PM Principles Behind These Tools
>
> - [ ] 1. **Clarity trumps perfection** - A clear decision made quickly beats analysis paralysis
> - [ ] 2. **Assumptions must be tested** - Document what you believe, then prove it
> - [ ] 3. **Decisions need context** - Future-you (and your team) needs to understand why
> - [ ] 4. **Frameworks guide thinking** - They're training wheels, not rules
> - [ ] 5. **User research is non-negotiable** - All frameworks assume you're talking to users
